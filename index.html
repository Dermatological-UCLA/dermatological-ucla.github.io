Our Story

What is the club about?

Dermatological is UCLA’s first premier undergraduate Dermatology student organization comprised of students ranging from pre business economics to pre health united by a passion for skin health and skin hygiene education.

What We Do

What’s the actual work you do?

To date, Dermatological has educated 7,400 students, adults, and healthcare community workers through our club formulated sun safety curriculum approved by Dermatologists. Dermatological primarily visits Title 1 underserved educational institutions to build skin health and hygiene literacy skills early, so young adults have the foundation to make informed decisions pertaining to their health in the future.

Partnerships

Who do you partner with?

Dermatological is infinitely grateful for the support that our sponsors provide- our partners range from nonprofit foundations such as the Claire Marie Foundation, to nonprofit clinics such as Viacare Community Health Center, to Los Angeles County support through the Department of Public Health and the Department of Mental Health, and finally well known skincare businesses such as SunBum, Isdin, and La Roche Posay.
